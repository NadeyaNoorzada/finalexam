black Pink Official Page

Blackpink is a Korean girl group consisting of Jisoo, Jenni, Rosie and Lisa under YG Entertainment. The group debuted in August 2016 with the single from their album, Square One, featuring the songs "Sweet" and "Bumbaya", their first number-one entries on South Korea's Gaon Digital Chart and Billboard's Global Digital Song Sales, respectively.



Members


Jennie

Jenny Kim, better known by her stage name Jenny, is a singer and rapper from South Korea. He entered the music world in 2016 as a member of the Blackpink group and under the YG Entertainment label.

Lisa

Elisa Manoban, better known by her stage name Lisa, is a Thai rapper, singer, and dancer based in South Korea. She is a member of the Korean girl group Blackpink under the YG Entertainment label.

Jisoo

Kim Ji-soo, better known by her stage name Jisoo, is a South Korean singer and actress and a member of the girl group Blackpink, formed in August 2016 by YJ Entertainment.

Rose

Rosanne Park, better known by her stage name Rosie, is a Korean-New Zealand singer and dancer based in South Korea.
